OCHA AI Prototypes

Exploratory, AI-assisted visualisations and prototypes

⚠️ Prototype repository – not for decision‑making. This site contains experimental and exploratory artefacts. Content is not validated, not authoritative, and not operational.

Purpose

This website provides a controlled environment for OCHA staff and partners to experiment with AI‑assisted “vibe coding” and to share early prototypes for learning and discussion.

It supports rapid innovation while maintaining transparency, accountability, and alignment with OCHA’s responsible AI principles.

Governance

All content in this repository is classified as Tier 1 – Shareable Prototypes under OCHA’s AI Vibe Coding Guidance.

Each prototype must clearly state its owner, purpose, data sources, AI involvement, and known limitations.

For details, see the repository files: GOVERNANCE.md and CONTRIBUTING.md.
